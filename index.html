Welcome to The Bogart Pool!

A member owned pool with a family friendly environment

The Bogart Community Club was established in 1970 in an effort to give families an affordable place to cool off in the summer. Forty-four years later we are still following the original intentions of our founders. The Bogart Pool is owned and maintained by the permanent members. Each year a limited number of Permanent Memberships and Summer Memberships are available. Members are expected to conduct themselves appropriately and in accordance with the Club By-Laws. If you are looking for a family friendly and relaxed place to cool off in the summer we welcome you to fill out an application for membership. Summer Memberships are $385 for the 2020 season. If you'd like to apply for a membership please fill out the form below. If you have any questions fell free to email us!
